How to play

👁️

A rangoli pattern lights up on the board for a few seconds. Give it your full dhyan.

🫰

When it disappears, tap the exact tiles that were glowing to recreate it.

🪔

You have 3 diyas. Every wrong tap blows one out. Lose all three and you drop back one level.

🚫

From Level 6, red decoy tiles flash to confuse you. They were never part of the rangoli — don't tap them.

🪞

Every 5th level from Level 10 is a Mirror level — recreate the pattern flipped left ↔ right.

✦

Clear levels to earn Ratna. Spend them in the Shop on rangoli powder themes.
